Travel while pregnant in Tailwind Airlines

Expectant mothers can travel in Tailwind airlines upto 28 weeks

Single Pregnancies

Expectant mothers can travel from 28 weeks to 36 weeks in Tailwind airlines.

Multiple Pregnancies (twins, triplets etc)

Expectant mothers can travel upto 32nd week in Tailwind airlines.

  • A special form will be filled in at check-in.
  • The date of the doctor’s report can not be earlier than seven days.
  • The doctor who prepares the report must have the name, surname, diploma number and signature on the report.
