Atlantic Airways (Nasdaq Iceland: FO-AIR, Faroese: Atlantsflog) is the national airline of the Faroe Islands, operating domestic helicopter services and international passenger services as well as search and rescue responsibilities from its base at Vágar Airport, on the Faroese island of Vágar. Most of its pilots are members of the Faroese Pilot Association.

Travel while pregnant in Atlantic Airways

Expectant mothers may travel in Atlantic Airways before 38 weeks

With Atlantic Airways, pregnant passengers who are 0 to 34 weeks may travel without providing a medical certificate.

For 35 – 37 weeks, pregnant passengers should provide a medical certificate stated that pregnancy is normal

Pregnant passengers cannot travel from 38 + weeks

For more information, please visit https://www.atlantic.fo/en/travel-info/before-flying/booking/pregnant-passengers/
