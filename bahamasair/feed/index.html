]]> Excess Charges – International:

Bags greater than 50 lbs (23 kilos) & 62 inches.

51 – 70 lbs (23 – 32 kilos): $75.00
Greater than 70lbs (32 kilos) & less than 99 lbs: $125.00
Greater than 62″ & 70 lbs (32kilos): $150.00
Bags weighing more than 100 lbs will not be accepted.

Excess Charges – Domestic & Turks and Caicos:

Each Passenger is allowed two bags restricted to a combined weight of 44lbs (20kilos) and total circumference not greater than 62″ (width + height-breadth)

Allowable Bag greater than 62″: $65.00
Allowable Bag greater than 44 lbs: $65.00
Third Bag or More under 44 lbs: $50.00

 

For more details visit : http://www.bahamasair.com/Baggage/Baggage-Information
