SA Airlink (Pty) Ltd. is an airline based in Johannesburg, South Africa. It is privately owned and has developed into South Africa’s first feeder network aimed at linking the smaller towns, regional centers and hubs throughout South Africa. It was founded in 1978. It has code-sharing and schedule co-ordination agreements with South African Airways and South African Express.
