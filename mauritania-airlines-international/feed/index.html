]]> Mauritania Airlines International is an airline based in Nouakchott, Mauritania, serving as flag carrier of the country. The company was set up in 2010 in response to the demise of Mauritania Airways

Mauritania Airlines Carry-on Baggage Allowance

  • Mauritania Airlines offers you a franchise of 3 bags, each of 23 kg, for passengers traveling in the cabin Business and 2 luggage, each 23 Kg, for passengers traveling in Economy Cabin.
  • For domestic flights, a deductible of 30 Kg for passengers traveling in the Business cabin and 20 Kg for passengers traveling in Economy Cabin.

Mauritania Airlines Checked Baggage Allowance

In addition passengers will be allowed one piece of cabin baggage at a maximum weight of 15 lbs.(7 kgs.) and maximum overall dimensions of 21 inches x 14 inches x 8 inches (53 x 35 x 20 cms). These dimensions include wheels, all pockets and handles.

Mauritania Airlines Excess Baggage Allowance

For additional baggage check-in, charges will apply based on your itinerary.

For more details: https://www.mauritaniaairlines.mr/index.php/franchises-bagages
