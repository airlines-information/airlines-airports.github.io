]]> On airBaltic flights, each passenger (except infants) is allowed to carry on board

  • 1 cabin bag (55x40x20 cm) + 1 personal item (30x40x10 cm) with a Basic or Premium ticket;
  • 2 cabin bags (55x40x20 cm each) + 1 personal item (30x40x10 cm) with a Business ticket or PINS VIP card.

The total combined weight of the cabin bag and the personal item cannot exceed 8 kg for travelers holding Basic or Premium tickets.

]]> On airBaltic flights, the maximum weight of one piece of checked baggage is 20 kg, and the maximum dimensions of the bag are 100x50x80 cm.

BUSINESS – 2 bags with a joint weight of up to 40 kg free of charge.

PREMIUM – 1 bag (max. 20 kg) free of charge.

BASIC – from 19.99 EUR per bag per one way journey.

Checked baggage fees per bag per one way journey:

  • from 19.99 EUR* while booking tickets on airBaltic.com;
  • 34.99 EUR** once you already have a ticket via Manage my booking or during online check-in;
  • 35 EUR at travel agencies or airBaltic ticket offices;
  • 40 EUR at the airport check-in desk;
  • 60 EUR at the gate if the cabin bag is found to exceed the size and/or weight restrictions and has to be checked in.

* Checked baggage fees can vary depending on the selected route and flight duration. On connecting flights, a fixed fee of 29.99 EUR per bag one way journey is charged.

** 39.99 EUR on direct flights to Abu Dhabi (AUH), Tel Aviv (TLV) and Reykjavik (KEF).

If your trip includes a stopover in Riga exceeding 24 hours, the baggage fee will be applied to each flight separately.

PINS VIP members

  • with a Business ticket – 4 pieces (up to 80 kg) free of charge;
  • with a Premium ticket – 3 pieces (up to 60 kg) free of charge;
  • no free baggage allowance for Basic ticket holders.

PINS Executive members

  • with a Business ticket – 3 pieces (up to 60 kg) free of charge;
  • with a Premium ticket – 2 pieces (up to 40 kg) free of charge;
  • no free baggage allowance for Basic ticket holders.

For more details visit : http://www.airBaltic.com/Baggage/Baggage-Information
