]]> Royal Brunei Airlines offers a free check-in baggage allowance of 23kg for passengers of the Economy Class and 40kg for passengers of the Business Class.

Students travelling to the destination country of study or returning to their home country are given an additional 15kg of baggage allowance. Students must provide a valid student visa or Electronic Visa in the passport as well as a valid student ID/pass for verification at the check-in desk. This additional allowance applies to both Brunei and international students.Due to health and safety regulations, a single piece of luggage may not weigh more than 32kg.

Royal Brunei Airlines Sdn Bhd was established on 1974. It  is the national flag carrier airline of Brunei Darussalam, headquartered in the RB Plaza in Bandar Seri Begawan. It is wholly owned by the government of Brunei. Its hub is Brunei International Airport in Berakas, just to the north of Bandar Seri Begawan, the capital of Brunei.
