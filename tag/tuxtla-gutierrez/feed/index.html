AeroMexico is Mexico’s global airline which was founded in 1934. AeroMexico operates more than 600 flights daily with hubs at the Mexico City International Airport (Terminal 2) and at the Monterrey International Airport. AeroMexico route network spans 86 destinations on four continents, including 45 destinations in Mexico, 16 in the United States, 16 in Latin America, four in Europe, three in Canada, and two in Asia. Aeromexico and Aeromexico Connect are part of Grupo Aeromexico, S.A.B. de C.V., a holding company whose subsidiaries provide commercial aviation services in Mexico and the promotion of passenger loyalty programs.
