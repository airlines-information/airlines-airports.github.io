Air Creebec is a regional airline based in Val-d’Or, Quebec, Canada. It operates scheduled and charter services to 16 destinations in Quebec and Ontario. Its main base is Val-d’Or Airport, with a hub at Timmins/Victor M. Power Airport.The airline was established in June 1982 and started operations on 1 July 1982. At that time the Cree owned 51% of the company and Austin Airways owned the remaining 49%.
