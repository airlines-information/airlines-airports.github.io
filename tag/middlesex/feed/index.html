Travel while pregnant in Air Italy

Pregnant passengers / Expectant mothers can travel in Air Italy until 8 months of your pregnancy. For travel after 8 months, you need to provide a medical certificate stating that

  • the due date of delivery if expected within 4 weeks following the scheduled return flight
  • the pregnancy has had complications that will not permit travel;
  • the pregnancy is a twin birth.

* ticket change fees and penalties cannot be waived for pregnancy.

For more details, visit https://www.airitaly.com/en-en/beforetheflight/conditions.aspx#mamme
