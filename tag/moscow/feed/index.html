Travel during pregnancy in Air Astana

Pregnancy rules in Air Astana

  • Expectant mothers can travel in Air Astana upto 22 weeks without providing a medical certificate.
  • For single pregnancy, after 22 weeks until 34 weeks should provide a medical certificate which states that a pregnant mother is fit to travel.
  • For multiple pregnancy, after 22 weeks until 32 weeks should provide a medical certificate which states that a pregnant mother is fit to travel.
  • The validity of the certificate is 30 days.
  • Expectant mothers should come at check-in as earlier as possible (preferably to check-in opening time).

For more details, visit https://airastana.com/global/en-us/Information/Important-Notices/Information-for-Expectant-Mothers

Name change in Air Astana flight ticket

Misspelling the name in flight ticket

  • Passengers should send an application for correction in their flight ticket to call.centre@airastana.com.
  • Attach scanned color copy of the passport or ID card of the passenger.
  • Send the application to the above mentioned email before 24 hours of your departure

Full change of the first or last name in the Air astana flight ticket

  • Passengers should send an application for correction in their flight ticket to call.centre@airastana.com.
  • Attach scanned color copy of the passport or ID card of the passenger.
  • Send the application to the above mentioned email before 3 working days prior to your departure.

For more dteails, visit https://airastana.com/global/en-us/Information/Important-Notices/Name-Change
