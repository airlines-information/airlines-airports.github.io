Travel During Pregnancy in Jin Air

Expectant mothers can travel in Jin air till 32 weeks of their pregnancy.

Single pregnancy

32-36 weeks of pregnancy:

Expectant mothers who are between 32-36 weeks must submit a declaration during your travel.

Multiple pregnancy

Expectant mothers can travel in Jin air till 32 weeks of their pregnancy. After 33 weeks, travel is restricted with Jin Air according to IATA Medical Guidelines for pregnant women.
