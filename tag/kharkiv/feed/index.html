Ukraine International Airlines (UIA), PJSC is the flag carrier and the largest airline of Ukraine. The airline was established on 1992, with its head office in Kiev with its main hub at Boryspil International Airport outside of Kiev. It operates domestic and international passenger flights and cargo services to Europe, the Middle East, the United States and Asia.

Travel during pregnant in Ukraine International Airlines (UIA)

Expectant mothers can travel in Ukraine International Airlines until 35th week if it is single pregnancy and 32 nd week if multiple pregnancy.

  • parturient women can travel after delivery within 7 days.
  • newborn babies can travel under the age of 7 days.

If there is any complication, a medical certificate is required to travel in Ukraine International Airlines.

Ukraine International Airlines Medical form (MEDIF)

For more details, visit https://www.flyuia.com/ua/en/information/passengers-with-special-needs

*The medical certificate must be issued at most 7 days before the date of the beginning of the air carriage from the airport indicated on the ticket.
