Lost and Found contact – Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport

Lost and Found Office location: Rental Car Center on Level 2

Lost and Found Office hours: Monday – Friday 8:30 a.m. – noon and 1 – 4 p.m., . The office is closed on Saturdays, Sundays, and Broward County holidays.

Outside of normal business hours and on holidays: Airport Operations Control Center: 954-359-1210.

  • Items lost on an aircraft or an item checked-in on a ticketed flight, please contact the airline. The FLL Lost and Found Office does not accept these items.
  • Items lost in any other area in the airport including the security checkpoints and shuttle buses, please complete the Lost Item Claim form.
  • Items lost at a security checkpoint is turned over to our Lost and Found Office by TSA the next business day.
