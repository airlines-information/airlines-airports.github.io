AeroMexico is Mexico’s global airline which was founded in 1934. Aeromexico and Aeromexico Connect are part of Grupo Aeromexico, S.A.B. de C.V., a holding company whose subsidiaries provide commercial aviation services in Mexico and the promotion of passenger loyalty programs.
