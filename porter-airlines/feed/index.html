Porter Airlines is a regional airline headquartered at Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport on the Toronto Islands in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It was established on 2006. Owned by Porter Aviation Holdings, formerly known as REGCO Holdings Inc., Porter operates regularly scheduled flights between Toronto and locations in Canada and the United States using Canadian-built Bombardier Dash-8 Q 400 turboprop aircraft.

]]> Overweight or oversized baggage

Fee type Firm Flexible Freedom
Overweight fee or Oversize fee above 23kg (50lbs) $77.50 – to$89.13 $77.50 – to$89.13 $77.50 – to$89.13
  • Each bag weighing between 23 kg (50 lb) and 32 kg (70 lb) is charged a fee of $77.50 – $89.13 ($77.50 CAD/USD plus applicable taxes) per direction.
  • No single piece can weigh more than 32 kg (70 lb).
  • For 3rd and subsequent bags, no overweight or oversized bag fee is charged.
  • Any single piece measuring more than 158 cm (62 in) total dimension (the sum of the length, width and height) is charged $77.50 – $89.13 ($77.50 CAD/USD plus applicable taxes)
  • No single piece can exceed 203 cm (80 in) total dimension.
  • If a bag is both overweight and oversized, the $77.50 fee is charged only once.
  • For 3rd and subsequent bags, no overweight or oversized bag fee is charged.

*Your standard carry-on bag is free. If your carry-on is overweight or oversized, it will be checked at the gate for a fee starting at $47.50 to $129.38, taxes included.

Porter Airlines Lost and Found contact

Porter Airlines : Lost item at Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport (BBTCA)

If you have lost your item at Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport (BBTCA), please contact the below number or email

The Airport Lost and Found is located on the Departures level, to the right of Security B.

Toll-free: 1-844-617-4695

Email: BBTCAFM@JCI.com

Lost item at an airport other than Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport (BBTCA)

If your item was lost at an airport, please contact the appropriate airport Lost and Found department directly.

Lost item on Porter Airlines flight

If you lost an item onboard a Porter Airlines flight, please include the following information with your inquiry at lost.found@flyporter.com or 647-826-7094.

Your name, Your contact telephone number or email, Detailed description of lost item, Date item was lost, Flight and reservation number.

For lost electronic items, please also include the following, if available:

Model, Serial number, IMEI, PIN, Telephone number of lost mobile phone

Retrieving lost property at the Porter Lost and Found

Porter Lost and Found location: Domestic Baggage Hall on the Arrivals level

All found items can be retrieved at the Porter Lost and Found. Please present your photo ID to the agent.

Hours of operation for the Porter Lost and Found: Monday to Friday 7:00 am – 10:00 pm EST; Saturday 9:00 am – 6:00 pm EST; Sunday 10:00 am – 10:00 pm EST

If you would like to have your item delivered to you by courier, this may be arranged at your own cost. Please provide:

FedEx account number, Delivery address, Contact telephone number
Estimated value of item (for customs/border crossing purposes)

Lost item on other air carriers

If your item was lost onboard a flight not operated by Porter Airlines, please contact the appropriate air carrier.
