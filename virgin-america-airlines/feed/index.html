Virgin America Airlines Inc. is an American airline that was founded in 2004 and began operations in 2007. Headquartered in Burlingame, California in the San Francisco Bay Area, with San Francisco International Airport as its main hub, Virgin America Airlines is a brand that was created by the British Virgin Group as an American counterpart to the corporation’s other airlines. The airline’s mission statement entails providing low-fare, high-quality service for service between major metropolitan cities on the Eastern and West Coast seaboards. Virgin America Airlines’s frequent flyer program Elevate provides award flights and other benefits to frequent fliers.

]]> Excess Weight Charges (applied in addition to the checked bag fee):

  • The fee for bags 1-10 weighing more than 50 up to 70 pounds is $50.00.

For all bags:

  • The fee for bags 1-10 weighing more than 70 up to 100 pounds is $100.00.
  • Interline guests may not check a bag weighing more than 70 pounds.
  • Any bag weighing more than 100 pounds will not be accepted for check in at the airport. The Guest will be directed to call the local freight facility.
