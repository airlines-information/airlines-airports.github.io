]]> Procedure / Documents required to obtain Travel certificate from GoAir

Travel Certificate may be required for your recent trip / to apply LTA / if you misplaced your boarding pass.

You can get travel certificate from GoAir either by online or email or by visiting GoAir offices

Procedure to get GoAir travel certificate online

*Travel Certificate will be issued for the entire booking and not for select sectors.

  • Go to https://www.goair.in/services/travel-certificate
  • Enter PNR number
  • Enter Email
  • There may be service charge of ₹200 to retrieve the certificate which may be non-refundable

(or)

Visit any nearby GoAir Office to obtain GoAir travel certificate

Call GoAir Customer care number for any queries

1800 2100 999 /+91 22 6896 8300 | Fog Helpline: +91 22 6868 6000

]]> Procedure / Documents required to obtain Travel certificate from Air India

Travel Certificate may be required for your recent trip / to apply LTA / if you misplaced your boarding pass.

You can get travel certificate from Air India either by online or email or by visiting Air India offices

Procedure to get Air India travel certificate online

  • Go to http://www.airindia.in/index.htm
  • Enter PNR number
  • Enter Email
  • There may be service charge to retrieve the certificate which may be non-refundable

Procedure to get Air India travel certificate by email

For forwarding scanned copies of travel documents / Updating FFP mileage, Email traveldocs@airindia.in to retrieve the Air India travel certificate.

(or)

Visit any nearby Air India Office to obtain Air India travel certificate

Call Air India Customer care number for any queries
