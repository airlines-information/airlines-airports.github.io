]]> Mokulele Flight Service, Inc., doing business as Mokulele Airlines, Inc., is an American commuter airline based in Kona on the Big Island of Hawaii. The airline operates scheduled inter-island and charter flights primarily among Hawaii’s smaller airports. The airline was founded in 1994 as Mokulele Flight Service by Rebecca “Kawehi” Inaba, and was the first airline in Hawaii to be founded by a Native Hawaiian woman.
