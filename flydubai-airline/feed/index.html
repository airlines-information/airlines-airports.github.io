Once you get approval from your embassy or from the government, book the repatriation flight through FlyDubai from Dubai to the following destination:

  • Bangladesh (Dhaka)
  • Bulgaria (Sofia)
  • Egypt (Cairo, Alexandria)
  • Finland (Helsinki)
  • Georgia (Tbilisi)
  • India (multiple airports)
  • Iran (Lar)
  • Kyrgyzstan (Bishkek)
  • Pakistan (multiple airports)
  • Romania (Bucharest)
  • Serbia (Belgrade)
  • Ukraine (Kyiv)

*the above listed countries who are resident in or visiting the UAE can now book special repatriation flights

Get ICA Approval from UAE while you return to Dubai, UAE

Residents or nationls of UAE who wants to return to Dubai must obtain ICA (Federal Authority For Identity And Citizenship) approval to enter into UAE. Click here for how to get ICA Approval

Documents required for travelling to Dubai

Passengers need to submit the health declaration form and the quarantine undertaking form to the Dubai Health Authority on arrival in Dubai.

*For undertaking form you need to provide Name, Passport / ID No, Mobile number,  Number of friend/sponsor/next of kin, Email address

For repatriation flight, FlyDubai has reduced the baggage allowance for the passenger

Hand luggage allowance: 7kg

Check https://www.flydubai.com/en/plan/covid-19 for more details

Terms and conditions

  • All flights are only for repatriation and will carry citizens of the above-mentioned countries who currently reside in or are visiting the UAE.
  • All flights offer one-way Economy Class fares originating in Dubai.
  • The offered fare is inclusive of 20 kg checked baggage allowance.
  • Cabin baggage will be limited to laptop, handbag, briefcase or baby items.
  • All flights will operate from Terminal 2 at Dubai International (DXB).
  • All flights are subject to government approvals and will operate only if approvals have been obtained.
  • No meals will be served during the flight, but a snack box will be provided.
  • No penalty will apply in case of flight modification or cancellation.
  • Any applicable refunds will be processed in the form of a flydubai voucher.
  • If you do not travel on the flight you have booked, please note that the ticket is non-refundable and non-changeable.

For more details, visit https://www.flydubai.com/en/contact/operational-updates/repatriation-flights
