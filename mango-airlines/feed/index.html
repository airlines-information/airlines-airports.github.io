Travel while pregnant in Mango Airlines

Expectant mothers can travel upto 35th week in Mango Airlines.

For further information please contact Mango Airlines Medical Reservations Department on 086 10 10 214

Single Pregnancies

Less than 28 Weeks pregnancy in Mango Airlines: No forms required provided they do not have any complications. In the event of complications, a medical certificate stating that the Guest is fit to travel by air will be required.

Between 28-35 Weeks pregnancy in Mango Airlines: A medical certificate stating that the Guest is fit to travel by air will be required. The Guest must carry a copy of the medical certificate, on person, during travel.

From 36 Weeks pregnancy in Mango Airlines: MANGO does not permit Guests that are 36 weeks or more into their pregnancy to travel.

Multiple Pregnancies

Less than 28 Weeks pregnancy in Mango Airlines: No forms required provided they do not have any complications. In the event of complications, a medical certificate stating that the Guest is fit to travel by air will be required.

Between 28-31 Weeks pregnancy in Mango Airlines A medical certificate stating that the Guest is fit to travel by air will be required. The Guest must carry a copy of the medical certificate, on person, during travel.

From 32 Weeks pregnancy in Mango Airlines: MANGO does not permit Guests that are 32 weeks or more into their pregnancy to travel.
