]]> Air Cote d’Ivoire is the flag carrier of Ivory Coast, based in Abidjan. The company succeeded the country’s former flag carrier Air Ivoire, which went bankrupt in 2011. It started operations on 12 November 2012.

The airline was set up on 15 May 2012, as a private-public entity, partially owned by Air France – which intended to make Port Bouet Airport a regional hub – and the Aga Khan Fund for Economic Development (AKFED).

Traveling while Pregnant in Air Cote dIvoire airline

  • If you are 28th week of pregnancy, your obstetrician must issue a medical certificate to travel, indicating the number of weeks of pregnancy and the expected date of birth.
  • This certificate must be issued to you at the latest 7 days before departure.
  • You can travel to the end of your 32th week. At 32 weeks 1 day, travel is prohibited for pregnant women.

For more details: https://www.aircotedivoire.com/en/infos-pratiques/femme-enceinte/

Traveling with Pets in Air Cote dIvoire airline

  • Pets are accepted in cabin provided they are housed in a secure container (bag or cage) of 55x40x23cm (L x W x S) dimensions and a total weight not exceeding 5kg.

For more details: https://www.aircotedivoire.com/en/infos-pratiques/faire-voyager-vos-animaux/
