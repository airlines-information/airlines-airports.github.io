Montenegro Airlines a.d. (Montenegrin: Монтенегро ерлајнс / Montenegro erlajns) is the flag carrier of Montenegro, headquartered in Podgorica. It operates scheduled and charter services throughout Europe from its hub at Podgorica Airport with a second base maintained at Tivat Airport. It is as of June 2019 the only Montenegrin airline offering regularly scheduled services.

On Montenegro Airlines flights, you can carry with you one piece of uncluttered luggage with a maximum weight of up to 8kg, and whose sum of dimensions (including wheels, handles and outer pockets) does not exceed 115cm (eg 55x40x20 cm).

Montenegro Airlines Checked Baggage Allowance

  • Max weight 32 kg apiece
  • Max size 158 cm * per piece
    (sum of 3 dimensions)

Montenegro Airlines Excess Baggage Allowance

In case your luggage does not meet the conditions stated in the part allowed for free luggage in terms of number of pieces, weight or dimensions for extra luggage is provided.
