Travelling With Pets in Air Malta

You have three options to carry your pet on Air Malta scheduled flights:

  • Pets carried in the passenger cabin (PETC) – only cats & dogs up to 10 kgs including carrier
  • Pets as Accompanied Checked in Baggage (AVIH) – cats, dogs, hares, rabbits, ferrets and birds up to 32kgs including carrier
  • Pets in Cargo hold
