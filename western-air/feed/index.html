Western Air Limited is the largest privately owned airline in the Bahamas. Western Air has its headquarters on the grounds of San Andros Airport in Andros Island, Bahamas. The airline was established in 2001 by husband-and-wife team, Rex J. Rolle and Shandrice Woodside- Rolle. Currently, Mr. and Mrs. Rolle serve as president & CEO and COO respectively. Western Air now operates from their new second hub at Grand Bahama International Airport. Which will serve as its main northern hub. opening new direct routes to direct flights to Haiti, Jamaica, Turks & Caicos, and eventually Fort Lauderdale and West Palm Beach, Florida.

Excess Baggage Allowance:

  • Baggage in excess of 40lbs but less than 100lbs. will be subject to a charge of $1.80 per pound (VAT included).
  • Second excess bag up to 40lbs or 40” L x 24”W x 18”H will be subject to a charge of $50 (VAT included).
  • Third excess bag up to 40lbs or 40” L x 24”W x 18”H will be subject to a charge of $70 (VAT included)
  • Additional excess baggage more than 40lbs or 40”L x24”W x 18”H but less than 70lbs will be subject to a charge of $100.00 (VAT included).
  • Oversized and overweight bags weighing 70lbs -100lbs or 48”Lx 30”Wx 24”H will be subject to a charge of $125.00 (VAT included).

For more details visit : http://www.westernair.com/Baggage/Baggage-Information
