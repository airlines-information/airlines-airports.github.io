The gateway to Western Australia Perth Airport Pty Ltd plays a significant role in the economic, social and cultural activities of this State by facilitating travel and employment, connecting people and places, and providing support for communities.

It is the fourth busiest airport in Australia in terms of passenger traffic, and operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week throughout the year. Almost 14 million passengers travelled through Perth Airport during the 2014/15 financial year. Perth Airport is part of a 2,105 hectare estate with sufficient capacity to expand and meet projected commercial aviation demands for the next 40 years and beyond.

Baggage Lockers / Lost and Found contact – Perth Airport, Australia

Baggage LockersBaggage TrolleysLost and Found Services
Baggage Lockers Services at Perth Airport: Lockers are available at all terminals for short-term storage of luggage up to six weeks. Simply secure your bag/s inside a locker and make payment.

Smarte Carte at T1 International
Open hours: 24/7
For enquiries, please contact Smarte Carte.

Baggage Locker locations:

    • T1 International – within the Short Term Car Park nearest the public Arrivals Hall
    • T1 Domestic and T2 – on the pathway between T1 and T2
    • T3/T4 – along the walkway between the terminal and the Short Term Car Park
Baggage Trolleys:
Baggage trolleys are available at all terminals. If you’re arriving internationally, or using our Short Term Car Parks, you can find a trolley to use free-of-charge.

Baggage Trolley locations:

  • T1 International, Baggage Reclaim (Free)
  • T1 Forecourt ($4.00)
  • T1 Short Term Car Park (Free)
  • T1 Domestic, Baggage Reclaim ($4.00)
  • T3/T4 Forecourt ($4.00)
  • T3 International Baggage Reclaim (Free)
  • T4 Ground Floor ($4.00)
Lost & Found:

Items lost at Terminals, Car Parks or Buses:

  • If any items lost at Perth Airport Terminals as well as in car parks or on the terminal transfer or Long Term Car Park buses.
  • For any lost services, please register on online .

Items lost at aircraft:

  • If any items left on the aircraft, please contact your airline directly.

If any enquiries about items lost on-board Qantas or QantasLink flights, please contact

  • Phone: +61 (0)8 9270 9504
  • Open hours: 9.00am and 1.00pm WST, Monday to Friday.

If any enquiries about items lost on-board Virgin Australia flights, please contact

  • Phone: +61 (0)8 6168 6635
  • Open hours: 9.00am – 5.00pm (midnight) WST, seven days a week.

If you want to register about any lost items or delayed/damaged baggage, please register online
Lost Property opening hours: 8.00am – 4.00pm WST, Monday to Friday.
