Special Assistance in Pegasus Airlines

Traveling while Pregnant in Pegasus Airlines

  • Pegasus Airlines, flying until the 36th week of pregnancy, would not cause any harm in single pregnancies.
  • In multiple pregnancies, the duration is restricted to 32 weeks.

For more details: https://www.flypgs.com/en/travel-glossary/pregnant-passenger

Traveling with Pets in Pegasus Airlines

  • Pegasus only allows the carriage of cats, dogs and birds on domestic Scheduled Flights and cats and dogs on international Scheduled Flights.

For more details: https://www.flypgs.com/en/write-to-us/pets#sf-2
