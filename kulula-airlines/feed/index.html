Travel while Pregnant with kulula Airlines

Soon to be super-moms from 28 weeks pregnant are required to bring along a medical certificate from a doctor or midwife confirming the approximate due date and that there are no complications with her pregnancy. A pregnant passenger may NOT travel with us after:

  • The end of 36 weeks if pregnant with one baby
  • The end of 32 weeks if pregnant with more than one baby

kulula Airlines Checked Baggage Allowance

  • If your bag weighs over 20kg but less than 32kg, a heavy bag fee of R330 will apply at the airport
  • Bags weighing more than 32kg and are over the maximum dimensions of 75 x 43 x 90cm each cannot be checked in and must be sent as cargo at your own expense
  • If a bag is shared between two or more travellers, it must still conform to the 20kg limit per bag

kulula Airlines Excess Baggage Allowance

  • Extra bags are not refundable after check-in. To cancel your purchase call our Contact Centre 0861 KULULA (585852) more than 2 hours before your flight departure
  • If a bag is shared between two or more travellers, it must still conform to the 20kg limit per bag
  • If you’ve checked in online please drop your bag at the bag drop-off counter at least 45 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior to departure for international flights

]]> Heavy bags

Any checked baggage on a kulula flight weighing more than 20 kg a piece is considered as a heavy bag. Heavy bags are charged a fee of R300 each at the airport.

Bags that weigh more than 32 kg and over the maximum dimensions of 75 x 43 x 90 cm each will be transported as cargo at your own cost and will not be carried on your flight.

Buy extra bags

Weigh your bag before leaving for the airport. If it weighs over 20 kg you can book or add an extra bag online at R290 or by calling our Contact Centre on 0861 KULULA (585852) at least 2 hours before departure. Any additional pieces of baggage checked-in will cost an extra R385 at the airport.
