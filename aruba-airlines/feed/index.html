]]> Arubaanse Luchtvaart Maatschappij N.V., operating as Aruba Airlines, is an airline headquartered in Oranjestad, Aruba. Established in 2006, it has a fleet of four aircraft and 300 employees as of July 2016. The airline is planning to expand its operations in 2017. The airline was founded on 2006 and began charter operations with a seven-seat Piper PA-31 Navajo in 2010. The main headquarters is in the city of Oranjestad in Aruba, which is the main operation centre, and the airline opened a new office at Queen Beatrix International Airport.
