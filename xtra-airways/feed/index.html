]]> Applicable tariffs on excess and oversize baggage

  •  Baggage in excess of the provisions of Section A above may be carried subject to a charge of $50.00 for each piece up to seven pieces. Seven pieces is the maximum number of excess pieces allowed per passenger.
  • Each oversize piece, the linear dimensions of which exceed 62 inches, but not more than 80 inches, and/or the weight of which exceed 50 pounds, but does not exceed 100 pounds will be charged $50.00 each in addition to any applicable excess baggage charges.
  • XP will not accept baggage exceeding 62 inches in overall linear dimensions or exceeding 100 pounds.

For more details visit : http://www.xtra airways .com/Baggage/Baggage-Information

]]> According to Xtra Airways’ baggage policy, each ticketed passenger is entitled to transport as checked baggage free of charge one bag up to 50 pounds and 62 inches (total linear dimensions – length plus width plus height).

Passengers may also carry on without charge, one – additional bag totaling no more than 45 inches in overall linear dimensions, and weighing no more than 40 pounds, which is capable of being stowed under a passenger seat, in an overhead compartment, or in an empty passenger seat.
